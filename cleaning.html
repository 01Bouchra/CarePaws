Cleaning & grooming tips

Keeping your pet clean prevents illness, reduces shedding, and keeps them comfortable. Groom regularly, trim nails, and bathe only when needed using gentle pet products. Clean their ears and brush their teeth regularly to avoid infections.

Keeping your home clean is just as important. Scoop the litter box daily and change it completely once a week, wash food and water bowls regularly, vacuum pet hair often, and wash bedding weekly. These simple habits make life more pleasant for both you and your pet.

Grooming tips :

  • Brushing: Brush cats 2–3 times a week; dogs 2–7 times depending on coat type.
  • Bathing: Bathe only when needed, using gentle pet shampoo.
  • Nail Care: Trim nails every few weeks, carefully to avoid hurting your pet.
  • Ears &Teeth : Clean ears gently; brush teeth a few times a week to prevent infections.

Keeping your home clean is just as important. Scoop the litter box daily and change it completely once a week, wash food and water bowls regularly, vacuum pet hair often, and wash bedding weekly. These simple habits make life more pleasant for both you and your pet.

Cleaning advice for your house and furry :

  • Litter Box: Scoop daily and fully change litter once a week.
  • Bowls & Toys: Wash food and water bowls, and clean toys regularly..
  • Hair & Bedding: Vacuum pet hair often and wash bedding weekly.

Keeping your pet clean and groomed keeps them healthy and happy. Simple weekly habits make life easier for both you and your pet
